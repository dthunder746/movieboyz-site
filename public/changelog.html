What's new

24 Feb 2026
Click table rows to plot movies on the chart

Select any movie in the table to see its profit line on the chart. Select multiple movies to compare them side by side. A "Clear selection" button appears above the table while rows are selected, and the chart heading updates to show which movie(s) you're viewing. Changing the owner filter automatically clears the movie selection.

Click leaderboard cards to explore an owner's movies

Click any owner card in the leaderboard to filter the table to their movies and switch the chart to show each of their movies as a separate profit line. Click the same card again to return to the full view. The owner filter buttons below the chart work independently and support multi-select ‚Äî click multiple owners to compare them.

Chart zoom and pan

Scroll the mouse wheel over the chart to zoom in on a time range. On mobile, use a two-finger pinch gesture. Click and drag to pan left or right. A "Reset zoom" button above the chart restores the full view.

Latest dates shown first in the table

The Daily Gross and Weekly Gross columns are now ordered with the most recent date on the left, so you see the newest numbers without scrolling right.

Chart tooltip sorted by profit

When hovering over the chart, owners are listed from highest to lowest profit at that point in time.

Mobile table scrolling fixed

On touchscreens, swiping left/right on the table now scrolls as expected instead of accidentally triggering column resize.
